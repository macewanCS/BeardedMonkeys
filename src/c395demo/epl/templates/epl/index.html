Documentation of the application:

CREATING A TICKET

1. Click the "Create Tickets" button at the top of the screen.

2. Select which of the ticket types you wish to submit.

3. Enter the requested information on the ticket creation page.

4. Click the submit button at the bottom of the screen to create the ticket.

VIEWING A TICKET

1. Hover over the "Manage/View Tickets" menu at the top of the screen.

2. Click "Manage My Tickets" to see a list of your own tickets, or "Manage Branch Tickets" for a list of all tickets within your branch.

3. Find the ticket that you wish to view. You may sort the list of tickets by ID, Author, Date, Category, or Status.

4. Click the "View" button in the ticket's options to view detailed information about the ticket.

EDITING A TICKET

1. Hover over the "Manage/View Tickets" menu at the top of the screen and click the "Manage My Tickets" button.

*You may also use "Manage Branch Tickets" if you are a manager.

2. Find the ticket that you wish to edit. You may sort the list of tickets by ID, Author, Date, Category, or Status.

3. Click the "Edit" button in the ticket's options.

4. Edit the entered information as desired.

5. Click the "Submit" button to submit the edited information.

RESOLVING/REOPENING A TICKET

1. Hover over the "Manage/View Tickets" menu at the top of the screen and click the "Manage My Tickets" button.

*You may also use "Manage Branch Tickets" if you are a manager.

2. Find the ticket that you wish to mark as resolved or reopen. You may sort the list of tickets by ID, Author, Date, Category, or Status.

3a. If marking the ticket as resolved, click the "Resolved" button in the ticket's options.

3b. If reopening the ticket, click the "Re-open" button in the ticket's options.

4. Click the "Yes" button in the pop-up window.
